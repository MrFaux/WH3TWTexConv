⚙️ Conversion Options & Guidelines
Normal Map Output

Standard Blue reconstructs standard normal coordinates. Raw Orange extracts raw DXT5nm channels as-is.

DirectX inverts the green channel (Y-). Only relevant for Standard Blue.

Material Map Output

Merged is a packed texture map. Channels splits it into metallic/roughness/AO grayscale files.

💡 Recommendations

Unsure what to use? Keep defaults.

  • OpenGL / Standard Blue is the default format used in 3D modeling programs like Blender, Substance Painter, and Asset Editor.
  • Raw Orange should only be exported if you're writing custom shaders or doing manual channel packing.
  • Export Both ensures you have both the pre-packed material map and individual channels for editing.
⚠️ Raw Orange mode selected: Normal maps will be extracted as-is in WH3's native DXT5nm format. They will look orange in image viewers and must be converted to blue before being usable in standard DCC tools.
⚠️ DirectX green-channel flip selected: The exported PNG will have its green channel inverted (Y- convention). Only select this if your project pipeline specifically expects DirectX normals (e.g. Unreal Engine, 3ds Max).
